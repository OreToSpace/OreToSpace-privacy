Privacy Policy – OreToSpace

Effective date: 2025-12-14
Developer: OreToSpace
Contact: oretospace@gmail.com

Summary

OreToSpace is a mobile game. We use third-party services for analytics and advertising to understand app performance and show rewarded ads. This policy explains what data may be collected, why, and how it is used.

Data we collect

The app may collect or share the following categories of data (directly or via integrated SDKs):

  • Device identifiers (e.g., installation identifiers, advertising identifiers where available).
  • Approximate location (typically derived from IP address, such as country/city level).
  • App activity (e.g., interactions with ads such as viewing or completing a rewarded ad).
  • Diagnostics (crash reports, performance data, and related technical information).

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect your name, email address, phone number, or precise location through the game.

Why we collect this data

  • Analytics: to understand gameplay usage and improve stability and performance.
  • Advertising: to show rewarded ads and measure ad performance.
  • Fraud prevention & security: to help detect invalid traffic and protect the integrity of ads.

Third-party services

We use third-party SDKs that may process data as described above, including:

  • Unity Analytics (analytics and diagnostics).
  • Unity LevelPlay / ironSource Mediation (advertising and mediation partners).

These providers may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data sharing

We may share data with third-party advertising partners and service providers to deliver ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud. We do not sell personal information in the common sense of “selling” it for money.

Data security

Data sent by the app is transmitted using encrypted connections (e.g., HTTPS) where supported by the integrated services.

Children

The game is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your choices

Depending on your device settings and region, you may be able to limit ad personalization through Android system settings. You can also uninstall the app to stop further data collection from your device.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about privacy, contact: oretospace@gmail.com

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Effective date” at the top will be updated accordingly.
